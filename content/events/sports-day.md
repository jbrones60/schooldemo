Get ready for one of the most exciting days of the school year — **Annual Sports Day 2025** at Bright Future School!

📅 **Date**: Saturday, 22nd November 2025  
🕖 **Reporting Time**: 7:30 AM (Students), 8:00 AM (Parents)  
📍 **Venue**: School Playground & Athletics Track, Bright Future School

## Sports Events

### Individual Events
| Event | Category |
|---|---|
| 100m Sprint | All classes |
| 200m Sprint | Classes 5 and above |
| 400m Run | Classes 8 and above |
| Long Jump | Classes 5 and above |
| High Jump | Classes 7 and above |
| Shot Put | Classes 9 and 10 |

### Team Events
- **Cricket** – Inter-House Tournament
- **Football** – Inter-House Tournament
- **Kabaddi** – Inter-House
- **4×100 Relay Race** – All houses

### Fun Events (All Students Welcome)
- Sack Race
- Tug of War
- Spoon and Egg Race
- Lemon and Spoon Race

## For Students

- Students must wear their respective **house colours** on Sports Day.
- Report to your house coordinator by **7:15 AM** at the designated assembly point.
- Participate with the spirit of good sportsmanship, fair play, and enthusiasm.
- Refreshments will be provided for students during the event.

## For Parents

Parents and guardians are warmly invited to attend and cheer for their children. 

- Entry to the school ground is **free for parents and family members**.
- Please carry your school identity card or mention your ward's name at the gate.
- Refreshments will be available on the campus.
- Photography and videography of events are permitted.

Let the games begin! 🏆

*— The Physical Education Department, Bright Future School*
