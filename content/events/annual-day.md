We are delighted to announce the **Annual Day Celebrations 2025** at Bright Future School!

📅 **Date**: Monday, 15th December 2025  
🕕 **Time**: 5:30 PM onwards  
📍 **Venue**: School Auditorium / Main Ground, Bright Future School

## Programme Highlights

- Welcome address by the Principal
- Lamp lighting ceremony
- Cultural performances by students — Classical Dance, Western Dance, Music, Drama
- Special presentations by student achievers
- Prize distribution for academic and co-curricular excellence
- Recognition of meritorious students and dedicated faculty
- Vote of thanks and closing ceremony

## Information for Parents

- Parents and guardians are **warmly invited** to attend and celebrate with us.
- Please arrive by 5:15 PM to be comfortably seated before the programme begins.
- Entry passes will be distributed through the class teachers closer to the event date.
- Photography is welcome and encouraged; video recording with permission.
- Refreshments will be provided at the venue.

## For Students Performing

- All performing students must report to their respective teachers by **3:30 PM** on the day of the event.
- Costume and prop details will be communicated separately by your department teachers.
- Please come dressed in your designated costume for the event.

We look forward to an evening of celebration, talent, and pride for our Bright Future community!

*— The Principal, Bright Future School*
