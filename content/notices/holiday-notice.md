Dear Parents and Students,

This is to inform you that **Bright Future School will remain closed for Summer Vacation** from:

📅 **1st May 2025 to 31st May 2025** (both dates inclusive)

Classes will **resume on Monday, 2nd June 2025**.

We wish all our students and their families a safe, restful, and enjoyable summer break. Please use this time for rest, recreation, family bonding, and light reading.

## Important Notes

- School transport will not operate during the vacation period.
- The school library and office will remain closed during holidays.
- For urgent enquiries during this period, please email us at info@brightfutureschool.edu.in and we will respond at the earliest.
- Dates are subject to change depending on academic calendar revisions. Please check this notice board for any updates.

## Reminders for Parents

- Please ensure your ward completes any holiday homework assigned by their class teacher.
- Keep a watch on this notice board for updates regarding the new academic year.

Have a wonderful summer!

*— The Principal, Bright Future School*
