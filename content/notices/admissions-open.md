Bright Future School is pleased to announce that **admissions for the academic year 2025–26 are now open**.

We welcome applications for the following classes:

- **Pre-Primary**: Nursery, LKG, UKG
- **Primary School**: Classes 1 to 5
- **Middle School**: Classes 6 to 8
- **High School**: Classes 9 to 10

**Seats are limited per section.** Parents are strongly encouraged to apply at the earliest to secure a seat for their child.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the school office or download the Admission Form from our [Downloads](/downloads) page.
2. Fill the form completely and attach all required documents.
3. Submit the form to the school office in person.
4. Our admissions team will contact you to schedule a campus visit or student interaction.

## Documents Required

- Birth certificate (original + photocopy)
- Aadhaar card copy of student
- 2 recent passport-size photographs
- Parent/guardian ID proof
- Address proof
- Previous school Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
- Previous year's report card (if applicable)
