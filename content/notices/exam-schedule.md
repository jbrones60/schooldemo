Dear Students and Parents,

The **Mid-Term Examinations for Term 2** are scheduled as follows:

📅 **Examination Dates**: 20th September 2025 – 28th September 2025  
🕗 **Student Reporting Time**: 8:00 AM sharp  
📄 **Hall Tickets / Admit Cards**: Will be distributed one week before the first examination

## General Examination Guidelines

- Students **must carry their hall ticket / admit card** on all examination days. Entry will not be permitted without it.
- Only permitted stationery (blue/black pen, pencil, ruler, eraser, geometry box) is allowed inside the examination hall.
- **Mobile phones and all electronic devices are strictly prohibited** inside the examination hall.
- Students must report to school at least **15 minutes before** the scheduled start time.
- Any form of malpractice will be dealt with strictly as per school policy.

## Subject-Wise Timetable

The detailed subject-wise examination timetable will be communicated to students via their class teachers and distributed as a printed circular. Parents are requested to collect a copy from their ward.

> **Note**: The timetable is being finalised and will be announced shortly. Please check this notice board and your child's school diary for updates.

## Preparation Guidance

- Students are advised to complete revision of all chapters covered in Term 2.
- Regular revision, practice of past question papers, and timely sleep are recommended.
- Students with any academic concerns are encouraged to approach their subject teachers for guidance well before the exams.

All the very best to our students!

*— The Principal, Bright Future School*
